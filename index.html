WE ARE FC KOVALAM

“Football is market, goal is our target”

At FOOT BALL KOVALAM, we believe that football is more than just a game – it's a way of life that begins with fun, grows with discipline, and thrives on togetherness. From our tiny Under-5 star, who take their first playful steps on the sand, to our Under-10 and Under-14 champions, sharpening their skills with energy and joy, every age group trains like a true football family. By the time they reach Under-17 and Above, they're not just players – they’re leaders, role models, and passionate athletes ready to represent Kovalam with pride.
