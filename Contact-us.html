Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Tickets can be purchased online through our website, at the stadium box office, or through authorized ticket partners. We recommend purchasing in advance as popular matches often sell out quickly.
Youth tryouts are open to players aged 8-18. Participants must complete a registration form, provide proof of age, and have a current physical examination. Check our Teams page for specific tryout dates and additional requirements.
We welcome corporate sponsors and community partners. Please contact our business development team at sponsors@sportland.com or call our main office to discuss partnership opportunities tailored to your organization's goals.
Player autograph sessions are typically announced on our Events page and through our social media channels. These sessions often coincide with home games or special community events. Sign up for our newsletter to receive notifications.
For canceled events, full refunds will be automatically issued to the original payment method. For rescheduled events, tickets will be valid for the new date, or refunds can be requested within 30 days of the announcement.
