About Us

FC Madras is a premier youth football club focussing on football development from the grassroots. We have a world class football academy with Elite Category accreditation from AIFF and a 4-star rating. Nestled in the picturesque coastal town of Mahabalipuram, Chennai (South India), we provide education, high-performance training, and recreational activities at our world-class residential facility. Our elite athlete program is a scholarship initiative, aimed at discovering the ‘Messi from Madras’.
